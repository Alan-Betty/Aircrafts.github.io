THE IDEA, TYPES AND INVENTION OF FLIGHTS

THE IDEA OF FLIGHT

Cayley defined the principles of mechanical flight and stated that, to fly, it was necessary for surfaces to be able to support a weight by applying "power to the resistance of air." In other words, the force that moved an object in a forward direction had to be greater than the opposite force (resistance or drag) that the air exerted on an object
